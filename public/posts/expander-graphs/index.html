Expander graphs are sparse graphs with strong connectivity properties. They show up across combinatorics, theoretical computer science, and number theory, and they serve as a bridge between random-like behavior and explicit constructions. This post will collect definitions, basic examples, and a few standard criteria (spectral gap and etc). My main motivations will come from number theory, so there will be inclinations towards that.
